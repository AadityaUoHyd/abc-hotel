About ABC-Hotel

Discover the essence of luxury and warmth at our premier hotel in Calangute, Goa.

Our Story

Nestled in the vibrant heart of Calangute, Goa, just steps from the bustling Calangute Market, ABC-Hotel is your gateway to an unforgettable Goan experience. Our mission is to blend world-class hospitality with the laid-back charm of Goa, creating a home away from home for every guest. Established with a vision to redefine luxury, we offer meticulously designed rooms, exceptional amenities, and personalized service that reflects the spirit of Goan warmth.

Located only 0.2 miles from Calangute Beach, our hotel is perfectly positioned for you to explore the golden sands, vibrant nightlife, and cultural landmarks like Fort Aguada and the Basilica of Bom Jesus. Whether you're here for a relaxing beach getaway, a romantic escape, or a family adventure, ABC-Hotel ensures every moment is memorable.

To provide unparalleled hospitality, combining modern luxury with the soulful essence of Goa, ensuring every guest leaves with cherished memories.
