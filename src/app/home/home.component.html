Top Places to Visit in Goa

Discover the vibrant culture and stunning landscapes of Goa, just moments away from ABC-Hotel. Explore these must-visit attractions to make your trip unforgettable.

Calangute Beach

Known as the "Queen of Beaches," Calangute offers golden sands, vibrant nightlife, and water sports like parasailing and jet-skiing.

Fort Aguada

A 17th-century Portuguese fort with stunning views of the Arabian Sea, featuring a historic lighthouse and scenic surroundings.

Basilica of Bom Jesus

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, this iconic church houses the relics of St. Francis Xavier and showcases Baroque architecture.

Dudhsagar Falls

One of India’s tallest waterfalls, Dudhsagar offers breathtaking views and adventure through jeep safaris and trekking.
